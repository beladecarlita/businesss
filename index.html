About Thee Luminary

Thee Luminary is a premium Cape Town and Johannesburg based talent and promotional staffing agency specializing in models, video vixens, event hostesses, bottle girls, promoters, and brand activation staff.

Our Vision

To become the go-to agency illuminating the major hubs of SA while creating sustainable opportunities for young creatives.
