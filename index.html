About Thee Luminary

We are a dedicated talent and promotional staffing agency operating in South Africa’s major entertainment hubs. Specializing in models, video vixens, event hostesses, bottle girls, promoters, and brand activation staff, we set a new standard of professionalism.

Our Mission

To professionalize South Africa’s nightlife and events industry by providing vetted, empowered, and brand-safe talent to leading brands, nightclubs, music producers, and event companies.

Our Vision

We aim to become the go-to agency illuminating Cape Town, Johannesburg, and soon Durban while creating sustainable opportunities for young creatives.
