About Thee Luminary

We are a premium, Cape Town and Johannesburg based talent and promotional staffing agency, specializing in models, video vixens, event hostesses, bottle girls, promoters, and brand activations. Launched recently we are on a mission to professionalize South Africa’s nightlife and events sector by providing vetted, empowered, and brand-safe talent to top clients like liquor brands, nightclubs, music producers, and event companies. Our goal? To become the go-to agency illuminating Cape Town, Johannesburg (and soon Durban) while creating sustainable careers and opportunities for young creatives — empowering you to shine while driving real impact in the R121 billion events industry!
